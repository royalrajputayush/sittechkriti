Logo Making Competition

This is a solo design challenge where participants will create a logo based on a theme revealed on the spot. Creativity, technique, and originality are key to standing out in this competition.


Event Structure

  • The event consists of a single round.
  • The theme will be disclosed at the venue on the day of the competition.
  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Participants must bring their own device.
  • Designs must be created using MS Paint only (Use of AI tools is prohibited).
  • Marking Criteria: Use of proper elements, construction, and overall appearance of the logo.
  • Final submission must be in PNG, JPG, or PDF format.

Special Theme

This event is sponsored by LookIntern. Participants will design a logo based on the theme provided by LookIntern, following the rules mentioned above.
