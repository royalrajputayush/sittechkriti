Poster Making Competition

A solo digital poster-making competition where participants have 1.5 hours to create a poster based on a topic assigned at the start of the event.

Rules

  • Individual participation only (no group entries).
  • Posters must be created digitally using Canva or Adobe Express.
  • Pre-made templates are prohibited; all designs must be original.
  • Posters must clearly and effectively represent the assigned theme.
  • Plagiarism or use of copyrighted material results in disqualification.
  • Participants must use their own laptop or tablet with required software installed.

Submission Guidelines

  • Posters must be saved in JPEG, PNG, or PDF format.
  • File naming convention: First Name + Registration Number (e.g., RANJAN22XXXXXXX14).
  • Late submissions will not be accepted.

Judging Criteria

  • Relevance to the Topic – How well the poster aligns with the assigned theme.
  • Creativity & Originality – Unique ideas and artistic execution.
  • Visual Appeal – Effective use of color, layout, and design elements.
  • Clarity & Presentation – Neatness and clarity of the design.

General Rules

  • Participants must carry valid college ID cards.
  • Internet access will be provided if required.
  • Unfair means will lead to disqualification.
  • Judges' decisions will be final and binding.

Entry Fee

No entry fee mentioned.
