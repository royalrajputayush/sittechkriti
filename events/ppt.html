PPT-Presentation

The PPT Presentation Competition at SIT_TECH_KRITI is a platform for individuals to showcase their knowledge, creativity, and presentation skills. Participants will deliver engaging presentations on various thought-provoking topics, applying innovative designs and persuasive communication techniques.

Rules

  • Team Size: Individual participation only.

  • Time Limit:
    • - Presentation Time: 7-10 minutes
    • - Q&A Session: 2-3 minutes

  • Submission Format:
    • - Presentations must be submitted in .ppt or .pptx format.
    • - Participants must share their presentations 20 hours before the event.

  • Plagiarism: Original work is mandatory. Plagiarized content will lead to disqualification.

  • Language: English (unless specified otherwise).

  • Judging Criteria:Participants will be evaluated based on the following aspects (Total: 100 marks):
    • - Content Relevance & Depth – 20 marks
    • - Design & Visual Appeal – 20 marks
    • - Presentation Skills – 20 marks
    • - Creativity & Innovation – 20 marks
    • - Handling Q&A – 20 marks

    • Suggested Topics


    Science & Technology:

    1. Future of Artificial Intelligence
    2. Space Tourism: Dream or Reality?
    3. Renewable Energy Innovations
    4. Nano-technology in Medicine

    Environment & Sustainability:

    1. Climate Change and Global Response
    2. Plastic Pollution and Alternatives
    3. Smart Cities: Sustainable Urbanization
    4. Wildlife Conservation Efforts

    Digital World:

    1. Cyber Security: Threats and Solutions
    2. Impact of Social Media on Mental Health
    3. Rise of the Metaverse
    4. Ethical Hacking: Pros and Cons

    Trending & Miscellaneous:

    1. Any topic related to Engineering
