Essay Writing Competition

A solo non-technical competition where participants will showcase their writing and critical thinking skills through an essay. The competition consists of two rounds.

Event Structure

Round 1

  • Topic: Provided in advance.
  • Duration: 1 hour.
  • Eligibility: Open to students from all branches and years.
  • Language: Choice of Hindi or English.
  • Selection Criteria: Top 10 students per language advance to Round 2.

Round 2

  • Topic: Given on the spot.
  • Duration: 1 hour.
  • Eligibility: Only selected 20 participants from Round 1 compete.

General Rules

  • College ID card is mandatory for participation.
  • Mobile phones and reference materials are prohibited during the writing period.
  • Maximum word limit: 800 words.
  • Writing sheets will be provided by the college.
  • Plagiarism results in immediate disqualification.

Judging Criteria

  • Relevance to the Topic: How well the content aligns with the prompt.
  • Originality & Creativity: Unique ideas and perspectives.
  • Clarity of Thought & Structure: Logical flow and coherence.
  • Grammar & Language: Accuracy in grammar and effective communication.
  • Overall Impact: How compelling and engaging the essay is.

Prizes

  • Top 3 participants will receive trophies and certificates.
  • All other participants will receive a certificate of participation.
