Yoga Competition

The Yoga Competition will feature Surya Namaskar and Advanced Yoga Posture demonstrations. Participants will showcase flexibility, stability, and presentation skills in a structured format.

Rules & Event Structure

Solo Surya Namaskar

  • Participants will perform 7 rounds of Surya Namaskar with upward and downward counting while maintaining proper posture.
  • Selected participants will proceed to 5 rounds of dynamic Surya Namaskar with mantra chanting, demonstrating precision and fluency.

Advanced Yoga Posture Presentation

Participants must demonstrate 5 advanced yoga postures, such as:

  • Chakrasana (Wheel Pose)
  • Ustrasana (Camel Pose)
  • Halasana (Plow Pose)
  • Sarvangasana (Shoulder Stand)
  • Tarasana (Star Pose)

Judging Criteria

  • Preparation
  • Presentation
  • Flexibility
  • Stability

Winners

First, Second, and Third place winners will be announced by the jury based on the overall performance.

Venue & Timing

Venue: Yoga Ground

Timing: 5:00 PM onward
