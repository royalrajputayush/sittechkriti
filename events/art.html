Art & Craft Club Competitions

Wall Painting Competition

Event Description

Participants will paint a wall based on one of the given themes, showcasing original artwork with no copied designs.

  • Team Size: 2–4 members.
  • Themes: Freedom, Technology & Innovation, Global Warming, Animal Cruelty, Madhubani Art.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Duration: 3 hours.
  • Size: Minimum 60 cm × 60 cm.
  • Allowed Materials: Colors & brushes will be provided.
  • Self-arranged Materials: Participants must bring drawing tools (pencil, eraser, etc.).
  • Mobile phones prohibited during the competition.
  • Judging Criteria: Uniqueness, message clarity, neatness.

Prizes

Winners will receive cash prizes or trophies, along with certificates.

Art Gallery

Event Description

Participants will physically present and explain their artwork, covering the idea behind the piece, medium used, and message conveyed. The artwork is not limited to paintings; participants may display handicrafts, sculptures, pottery, fiber art, decorative objects, etc..

Rules & Guidelines

  • Single round (additional rounds may be added based on participation).
  • Self-made artwork required.
  • Size: A3 (half chart paper) or A2 (full chart paper).
  • Judging Criteria: Artwork quality and explanation.

Prizes

Winners will receive cash prizes or trophies, along with certificates.
