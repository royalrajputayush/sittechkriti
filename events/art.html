Art Gallery Competitions

Art Gallery

Event Description

Participants will physically present and explain their artwork, covering the idea behind the piece, medium used, and message conveyed. The artwork is not limited to paintings; participants may display handicrafts, sculptures, pottery, fiber art, decorative objects, etc..

Rules & Guidelines

  • Single round (additional rounds may be added based on participation).
  • Self-made artwork required.
  • Size: A3 (half chart paper) or A2 (full chart paper).
  • Judging Criteria: Artwork quality and explanation.

Prizes

Winners will receive cash prizes or trophies, along with certificates.
