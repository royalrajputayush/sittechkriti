Bridge-O-Mania

Event Overview

The Bridzo-Menia competition challenges participants to build a structurally sound bridge using a limited set of materials. The event focuses on engineering design, load-bearing efficiency, and team collaboration.

Event Structure

  • Team Size: Maximum of 4 members per team.
  • Materials Provided:
    • 100 wooden ice cream sticks
    • Fevicol glue
  • Modifications Allowed: Sticks may be altered by cutting as needed for design adjustments.
  • Bridge Dimensions:
    • Length: 48–50 cm
    • Width: 11–15 cm
    • Height: 11–15 cm

Event Schedule

Bridge Construction & Testing

  • Date & Time: April 21, 2025, 11:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Venue: Room No. 106

Judging Criteria

  • Load-to-Weight Ratio: The highest load-bearing bridge (relative to weight) will be declared the winner.
  • Winners will be announced in 1st, 2nd, and 3rd positions.

Additional Notes

  • Each team must designate a group leader to coordinate activities.
  • Names on certificates will match registration form details.
  • All materials for model-making will be provided on the spot.

Prizes

  • Winners will receive certificates and awards based on performance.
