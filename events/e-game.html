  • Games: BGMI & Free Fire
  • Type: Gaming Tournament
  • Team Size: Solo / Duo / Triplet / Squad
  • Device: Mobile phones only

Event Structure: Free Fire

Round 1

Description:

  • There will be 4 groups: Group A, Group B, Group C & Group D.
  • Each group will have 12 teams.
  • In case of shortage, teams will be equally divided, and the number of groups may change as per coordinators' decision.
  • Each group will play one full Bermuda map.
  • 24 teams will be promoted to the next round based on performance.

Time Limit: As per map

Live Streaming: No

Judging Criteria:

  • 1 point per kill
  • 10 points for 1st position
  • 9 points for 2nd position
  • 8 points for 3rd position
  • 7 points for 4th position
  • 6 points for 5th position
  • 5 points for 6th position
  • 4 points for 7th position
  • 3 points for 8th position
  • 2 points for 9th position
  • 1 point for 10th position
  • No points for 11th and 12th positions

Elimination: Yes

Round 2

Description:

  • Top 6 teams from Group A and B will be merged to play 3 Bermuda maps.
  • Top 6 teams from Group C and D will be merged to play 3 Bermuda maps.
  • Top 6 teams from each merged group advance to next round.
  • Points table will be based on overall team performance.

Time Limit: As per map

Live Streaming: Yes

Judging Criteria: Same as Round 1

Elimination: Yes

Round 3

Description:

  • Winners from Round 2 will be merged and play 3 more Bermuda maps.
  • Final winners will be selected based on overall performance in Round 3.

Time Limit: As per map

Live Streaming: Yes

Judging Criteria: Same as Round 1

Elimination: Yes

Event Structure: BGMI (BattleGrounds Mobile India)

Round 1

Description:

  • There will be 2 groups: Group A and Group B.
  • Each group will have a maximum of 25 teams.
  • If there is a shortage, teams will be equally divided, and the number of groups may change as per coordinators’ discretion.
  • Each group will play one full Erangle map.
  • Top 20 teams overall will move to the next round based on performance.

Time Limit: As per map

Live Streaming: No

Judging Criteria:

  • 1 point per kill
  • 10 points for 1st position
  • 9 points for 2nd position
  • 8 points for 3rd position
  • 7 points for 4th position
  • 6 points for 5th position
  • 5 points for 6th position
  • 4 points for 7th position
  • 3 points for 8th position
  • 2 points for 9th position
  • 1 point for 10th position
  • No points for 11th to 25th positions

Elimination: Yes

Round 2

Description:

  • Top 20 teams from Groups A and B will be merged.
  • They will play 3 full maps: Erangle, Miramar, and Sanhok.
  • Top 16 teams based on overall performance will advance to the final round.

Time Limit: As per map

Live Streaming: Yes

Judging Criteria: Same as Round 1

Elimination: Yes

Round 3

Description:

  • Winners from Round 2 will play 3 more maps: Bermuda, Miramar, and Sanhok.
  • Final winners will be chosen based on overall performance across these maps.

Time Limit: As per map

Live Streaming: Yes

Judging Criteria: Same as Round 1

Elimination: Yes

General Rules

  • Participants must carry valid college ID cards.
  • Late entries will not be entertained.
  • Use of unfair means will lead to disqualification.
  • The decisions made by the judges and coordinators will be final and binding.
  • The decision to remake the game lobby is at the sole discretion of the Tournament Operator.
  • E-Gaming event coordinators will have control over the results of the tournament.

Judging Criteria

  • Based on eliminations and standings in match results.

Prizes

  • 1st Prize: Cash prize + Medal + Certificate
  • 2nd Prize: Medal + Certificate
  • 3rd Prize: Medal + Certificate
  • Most Kills (Final Round): Medal + Certificate
  • All Participants: Participation Certificate
