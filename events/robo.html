Robotic Exhibition

Event Overview

The Robotic Exhibition is a project-based competition where participants create innovative models based on Arduino and Macro Controller. Participation is open to SIT college students only, with teams comprising up to four members.

Event Structure

  • The competition consists of one round only.
  • Projects must be based on Arduino & Macro Controller.
  • Teams are required to bring their own kits/projects and demonstrate them on hardware.
  • Each team must present their projects during the designated presentation time.
  • Points will be awarded for both construction quality and presentation of the projects.

Rules and Notes

  • Each team must choose a group leader who will be responsible for all activities and communication.
  • Participants' names on certificates will match the registration form.
